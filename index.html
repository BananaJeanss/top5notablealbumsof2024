Album Details

WE DON'T TRUST YOU

by Future & Metro Boomin

We Don't Trust You is a collaborative album by Future and Metro Boomin in the trap genre, released on March 22, 2024. The album features guest appearances from The Weeknd, Travis Scott, Playboi Carti, and more notably Kendrick Lamar, who is featured on the song "Like That". The most notable song on the album is "Like That" featuring Kendrick Lamar, which is considered to be the reignition of the Kendrick-Drake feud, with disses aimed at Drake. As of June 2nd, 2025, the album has 2,374,094,775 streams on Spotify.


GNX

by Kendrick Lamar

GNX is the sixth studio album by Kendrick Lamar, in the hip-hop genre, released on November 22, 2024. GNX is his first album to be released after his departure from Top Dawg Entertainment and Aftermath Entertainment, instead being released through his own label, PGLang. The album has many notable songs, such as "Squabble Up" which was teased in the "Not Like Us" music video, "TV Off", "Peekaboo", "heart pt. 6" which aims to reclaim the "heart" series following Drake's diss "The Heart Part 6", and "Luther" featuring SZA.


Chromakopia

by Tyler, The Creator

Chromakopia is the eighth studio album by Tyler the Creator, in the hip-hop, R&B, and jazz genres, released on October 28, 2024. The album tackles multiple themes, such as anxiety with being a celebrity, his experience growing up, and life lessons featuring Tyler's mother, Bonita Smith. On October 23, 2024, Chromakopia: The World Tour was announced, with 53 dates across North America, and 24 dates in Western Europe, and the UK. The album was generally well received by critics, even though it was a shift from his previous genres.


BRAT

by Charli XCX

Brat is the sixth studio album by Charli XCX, in multiple genres such as electropop, club pop, hyperpop, dance, and electroclash, released on 7 June 2024. According to critics, the album was the highest-rated album of 2024 and the 15th highest rated album of all time, receiving a Metacritic score of 95. Notable songs on the album include "360" as the intro, "Von Dutch" as the lead single, "Apple" and "365" as the outro. On October 11, 2024, Brat and It's Completely Different but Also Still Brat was released as the remix album.

The Tortured Poets Department

by Taylor Swift

The Tortured Poets Department is the eleventh studio album by Taylor Swift, in the synth-pop, chamber pop and folk-pop genres, released on April 19, 2024. Taylor developed the album during her "The Eras Tour" in 2023, with the media scrutiny which inspired the record. The album broke multiple records, such as the most single-day and single-week streams for an album on Spotify. Critics praised Taylor's songwriting for emotional resonance and wit, but was also criticized for the album's length and lacking profundity.
